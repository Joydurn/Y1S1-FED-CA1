SPEEDRUNNING

“A speedrun is a play-through of a whole video game or a selected part of it (such as a single level), performed with the intention of completing it as fast as possible.” ~Wikipedia Definition

I have a huge interest in video games in general, but one thing I’ve been getting into is speedrunning which is fairly niche. When I played Resident Evil 2, a survival horror game that I loved, I decided to get into the speedrunning community of the game (my first speedrun game). I did this by watching streamers on the platform Twitch who also speedran the game, learning from them and meeting many great people along the way.


I made my way up the leaderboards on Speedrun.com, a website where speedrunners of all games compete on leaderboards to get the best times (world records), share their knowledge, or ask questions. Check out my profile here!

Eventually, I started to stream my speedruns on Twitch, a video streaming platform catered to gamers. Now with poly life taking over more of my free time, I don't stream too frequently anymore, but it's still fun whenever I do.
